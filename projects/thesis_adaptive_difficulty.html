Adaptive Game Difficulty (Unity, 2025)

Role: Solo · Focus: player modeling + difficulty control · Tech: Unity/C#

▶ Play on itch.io ↗ Repo

Problem

Keep players in flow without opaque difficulty spikes.

Approach

  • Composite skill score (PI) blends accuracy, pace (total & average wave time), and movement distance; players re-classified mid-session.
  • Playstyle axes: Movement vs Idle (MvI), Damage per Minute (DPM), and Weapon Switch Rate (WSR).
  • Targeted countermeasures mapped to skill × playstyle quadrants (spawn/spacing, “dart” enemies, cover spawns, swap locks/bonuses) with guardrails & smoothing.
  • 5-wave sessions: Waves 1–2 baseline; Waves 3–5 adaptions applied and updated within the same run.

What I built

  • Pluggable controller for a 3D wave-shooter prototype.
  • Lightweight HUD and hooks for session metrics.

Results (prototype)

  • Smoother challenge ramp; fewer sudden spikes observed in test sessions (qualitative).
