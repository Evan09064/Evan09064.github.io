Adaptive Game Difficulty (Unity, 2025)

Role: Solo · Focus: player modeling + difficulty control · Tech: Unity/C#

Problem

Keep players in flow without opaque difficulty spikes.

Approach

  • Collect KPIs (accuracy, TTK, deaths) and adjust spawns/aim/spacing with guardrails + smoothing.
  • Runtime debug UI to visualize KPIs and parameter changes.
  • A/B toggle (static vs adaptive) with lightweight telemetry.

What I built

  • Pluggable controller for a 3D wave-shooter prototype.
  • Debug overlays and perf checks to keep AI within frame budgets.

Results

  • Smoother challenge ramp and fewer spikes observed in test sessions (qualitative).
